Meet the Crew

Kavar

Founder · Master Barber

Trained in the Kurdish barbering tradition, Kavar brings 15 years of precision craft to Doune. Every appointment is an expression of his philosophy: the work is measured, the tools are warm, and the finish is exact.

Senior Barber

The Shop Space

Warm timber, calm mirrors, and clean geometry form a space designed for focus. You step in, noise falls away, and the appointment becomes the only thing in the room.

This is where Kurdish detail meets contemporary atmosphere: precise light, deliberate sound, and a station arranged for craft rather than speed.

Kurdish Craft TraditionThreading, ear singeing, and straight-razor work — passed through generations and practiced with exacting control.
